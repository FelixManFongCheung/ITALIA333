Haunting, then, can be construed as a failed mourning. It is about refusing to give up the ghost or - and this can sometimes amount to the same thing - the refusal of the ghost to give up on us. The spectre will not allow us to settle for the mediocre satisfactions one can gleam in a world governed by capitalist realism.
